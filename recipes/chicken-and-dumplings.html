Chicken And Dumplings

This recipe for easy chicken and dumplings could not be more simple and it tastes great too! You can mix in some dried herbs or sprinkle a bit of garlic or onion powder onto the biscuit dough to give the dumplings extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 3 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken meat
  • 2 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated biscuit dough

Steps:

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Stir condensed soup, chicken broth, and shredded chicken together in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until it begins to simmer.

  3. Step 3

    4. Cut each biscuit into quarters, and gently stir into the simmering soup.

  4. Step 4

    5. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until biscuits are no longer doughy in the center, 10 to 15 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve and enjoy!
